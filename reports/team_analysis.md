# 🏟️ Team Performance Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This section focuses on team-level performance including win rates, venue influence, and strategic decisions.

---

## 🏆 Top Teams

- Certain teams consistently dominate across seasons.
- High win percentage correlates with balanced batting and bowling.

---

## 🪙 Toss Strategy

- Toss-winning teams do not always win matches.
- Decision to field first shows slightly higher success in modern IPL seasons.

---

## 🏟️ Venue Performance

- Teams perform better in familiar home venues.
- Some venues favor high-scoring matches, others support bowlers.

---

## ⚖️ Close Matches

- Teams with strong finishers perform better in close matches.
- Death over performance is crucial in tight games.

---

## 📈 Seasonal Trends

- Team dominance shifts across seasons.
- New teams take time to stabilize performance.

---

## 📊 Conclusion

Team success depends on strategy, venue familiarity, and ability to adapt during different phases of the match.
