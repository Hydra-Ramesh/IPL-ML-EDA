## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🪙 Toss Impact
- Winning the toss does not guarantee match victory.
- Teams choosing to field first often have slightly better success in high-scoring venues.

---

### 🏟️ Venue Influence
- Certain venues consistently favor chasing teams.
- High-scoring grounds show higher success rates for aggressive batting teams.

---

### ⚡ Match Phases
- Powerplay overs (1–6): Moderate scoring, fewer wickets  
- Middle overs (7–15): Stabilization phase  
- Death overs (16–20): Maximum run acceleration  

---

### 📈 Run Trends
- Teams scoring heavily in death overs significantly increase win probability.
- Run rate acceleration after 15th over is a key success factor.

---

## 📊 Conclusion
IPL matches are influenced by multiple dynamic factors including venue, toss decisions, and scoring patterns across match phases. Teams that adapt strategy based on these factors tend to perform better.
