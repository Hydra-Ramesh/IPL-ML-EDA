# 🏏 Player Performance Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This section analyzes player performance across batting and bowling, including consistency, scoring patterns, and head-to-head matchups.

---

## 🔥 Top Batting Insights

- Top players contribute a major percentage of team runs.
- High strike rate players dominate death overs.
- Boundary percentage is a strong indicator of aggressive playstyle.

---

## 📈 Over-wise Batting Behavior

- Players generally maintain moderate strike rate in powerplay.
- Strike rate dips in middle overs due to field restrictions.
- Significant increase in strike rate observed in death overs.

---

## 🆚 Batter vs Bowler Analysis

Example: **Virat Kohli vs Jasprit Bumrah**

- Lower strike rate compared to overall average
- Higher dismissal frequency
- Indicates strong control by the bowler

---

## 🎯 Bowling Insights

- Economy rate is critical in middle overs.
- Wicket-taking ability in death overs is highly valuable.
- Some bowlers specialize in controlling specific batsmen.

---

## 🔄 All-rounders

- Players contributing in both batting and bowling provide high impact.
- All-rounders improve team balance and flexibility.

---

## 📊 Conclusion

Player performance varies significantly based on match phase and opponent. Head-to-head matchups reveal tactical advantages that can influence match outcomes.
